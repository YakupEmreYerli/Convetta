    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is my data safe? Are my files uploaded to a server?

    Your privacy is our priority. Most conversions (like PNG to JPG) happen directly in your browser. For complex formats like ICO or PDF, your file is processed on our server and is permanently deleted immediately after conversion. We never store or share your files.

    What image formats are supported for conversion?

    Our online image converter supports the most popular formats, including JPG, PNG, GIF, and WEBP. You can convert your files to and from any of these formats.

    Is this online image converter completely free?

    Yes, Convetta is a completely free online tool. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or usage limits for converting your images.

    Can I convert multiple images at once?

    Absolutely! You can select and convert multiple images at the same time. This bulk conversion feature allows you to process your images quickly and efficiently.

    Will the quality of my images decrease after conversion?

    We use advanced algorithms to ensure the highest possible quality. While some formats (like JPG) are naturally lossy, we minimize any quality decrease. For formats like PNG, the conversion is lossless, preserving the original quality.

    What is the maximum file size I can convert?

    To ensure fast performance for all users, there might be a reasonable file size limit for uploads. Most images intended for web use fall well within this limit.

    Do I need to install any software or application?

    No, you do not need to install anything. Convetta works entirely in your web browser. This makes the process fast, secure, and accessible from any device.

    What is the WEBP format and what are its advantages?

    WEBP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. WEBP images are significantly smaller than JPGs and PNGs, leading to faster website load times.

    How can I download all my converted files together?

    After the conversion is complete, we provide a 'Download All as ZIP' button. Clicking this button will compress all your converted images into a single ZIP file for easy downloading.

    Does the converter work on mobile phones and tablets?

    Yes, our image converter is fully responsive and designed to work seamlessly on all modern devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

    Why use an online converter instead of desktop software?

    Online converters offer great convenience. There's no need for installation or updates, you can access them from anywhere, and they don't use up your computer's resources. They are perfect for quick and easy conversion tasks.

    How is my privacy protected during conversion?

    Your privacy is paramount. Since most conversions happen client-side (in your browser), your files don't even leave your computer. For server-side conversions, files are deleted instantly after processing. We do not log, store, or view your images.
